# Bonanno Writing Workshop: Marketing Strategy & Professional Templates

## Overview

Two deliverables for the Bonanno Writing Workshop freelance tutoring business:

1. **Advertising/marketing strategy** focused on direct client acquisition at full rate ($150-180/hr), bypassing platform middlemen (Wyzant, Outschool) that take 25-30% and force price cuts
2. **Professional branded templates** and an overhauled income tracking system that work together as a cohesive client management toolkit

## Context

- One current client (Will, direct at $150/hr, weekly Sundays, fully paid)
- Wyzant and Outschool have been tried extensively — oversaturation and price cutting make them unsustainable as primary channels
- Website live at bonannoworkshop.com (GitHub Pages, now HTTPS)
- Payment via Venmo (@bonannowriting) or Zelle
- Target: $1,500/mo supplemental income
- Sarah is a PhD student at CUNY Graduate Center, teaches at Brooklyn College, lives in Greenpoint Brooklyn, from Pleasantville NY (Hackley School '14)

## Part 1: Marketing Strategy

### Principle

Direct clients at $150-180/hr through bonannoworkshop.com. No platforms. Every dollar goes to Sarah. Word-of-mouth and hyperlocal presence are the channels.

### Immediate (April 2026)

- **Referral from Will's family.** Ask his mom for a testimonial (2-3 sentences) and whether she knows any other families. One warm intro from a Pleasantville parent is the highest-value lead possible.
- **Google Business Profile.** Free. Set up as "Bonanno Writing Workshop" with the tutoring category. Parents search "writing tutor near me" and "college essay help Brooklyn." Link to bonannoworkshop.com.
- **Nextdoor posts.** Greenpoint and Pleasantville communities. Not an ad — a short introduction: who you are, what you do, link to site.

### Short-term (April-May)

- **Facebook parent groups.** Pleasantville Moms, Park Slope Parents, Brooklyn parenting groups, Westchester homeschool networks. Share a useful writing tip (e.g., "3 things college admissions readers actually look for") with site linked in profile. Build presence before selling.
- **Referral incentive.** $25 off a session for both the referring family and the new client. Mention in welcome packet and session summaries.
- **Printed materials.** Simple business cards or flyers using the navy/gold brand. Place at Pleasantville Library, Mt. Pleasant Library, Brooklyn Public Library branches, local coffee shops and bookstores.

### Summer (June-August, admissions season ramp)

- **High school guidance counselor outreach.** Email introduction + a one-page PDF (designed to match the brand) they can share with families. Priority targets:
  - Hackley School (alma mater — personal connection)
  - Pleasantville High School
  - Horace Greeley High School
  - Brooklyn private schools (Packer, Saint Ann's, Berkeley Carroll)
- **Free college essay workshop.** 1-hour session at a library or community space. Demonstrate expertise, collect contact info, funnel to paid 1-on-1s. Pleasantville Library or Brooklyn Public Library would both work.
- **Testimonial collection.** After every few sessions, ask parents for a short testimonial. Add to the website's reviews section.

### Ongoing

- Keep the website's "Currently accepting X new students" banner updated — creates urgency
- Every satisfied parent is a marketing channel. Make it easy for them to refer (the referral discount + a simple "know someone who might benefit?" line in session summaries)

## Part 2: Professional Templates

### Design System

All templates match the bonannoworkshop.com brand:
- **Colors:** Navy (#1c2a3a), Gold (#c9a84c), Cream (#f8f5ef), White (#ffffff)
- **Fonts:** Cormorant Garamond (headings), Inter (body)
- **Logo:** mark-transparent.png (small mark) or logo-transparent.png (full logo)
- **Format:** Single-file HTML, printable to PDF from browser. No dependencies beyond Google Fonts.

### Template 1: Invoice

A fillable invoice sent monthly (or at package purchase).

**Fields:**
- Invoice number (format: BWW-YYYY-NNN)
- Invoice date and due date (net 15 from issue)
- Client info: parent name, student name, email
- Line items: date, service type (Academic Tutoring / Application Essays), duration, rate, amount
- Subtotal, package discount if applicable, total due
- Payment methods: Venmo @bonannowriting / Zelle
- Package balance (if on a package): "X of Y sessions used, Z remaining. Expires [date]."
- Footer with business name and contact

### Template 2: Welcome Packet

Updated, branded version of the existing draft. Structured as:

1. **Welcome message** — brief, warm intro (keep the existing personal touch about Pleasantville/Hackley)
2. **About / Qualifications** — credentials, condensed from existing draft
3. **Services** — Academic Tutoring and Application Essays descriptions (aligned with current website copy)
4. **How it works** — what to expect in sessions, the draft-submission process
5. **Pricing** — current rates, matching the website:
   - Per session: $150 (Academic) / $180 (Application)
   - Monthly (4 sessions): $540 / $650 (save 10%)
   - Semester (16 sessions): $1,920 / $2,300 (save 20%)
6. **Package policies:**
   - Packages require full upfront payment to receive the discount
   - Monthly packages expire 6 weeks from purchase
   - Semester packages expire 20 weeks from purchase
   - Unused sessions are forfeited upon expiration, no refunds
   - Packages may be shared between siblings in the same family
7. **Policies** — payment terms (net 15, Venmo/Zelle), cancellation (24hr notice, 50% late cancel fee, full no-show fee, one freebie), referral program ($25 off for both parties)
8. **Contact info** — email, website, Venmo handle

### Template 3: Session Summary

A short form sent to parents after sessions (or weekly).

**Fields:**
- Date, student name, session number (e.g., "Session 5 of 16")
- Service type
- Topics covered (free text)
- What the student worked on (free text)
- Next steps / recommendations
- Next session date (if scheduled)
- Package balance if applicable
- Referral note: "Know a family who could use writing support? Both families receive $25 off a session."

### Template 4: Receipt / Payment Confirmation

Simple confirmation sent after payment is received.

**Fields:**
- Receipt number (format: BWW-R-YYYY-NNN)
- Date received
- Client name
- Amount received
- Payment method
- Invoice(s) covered
- Remaining package balance if applicable
- Thank you line
